OUR JOURNEY TOWARDS ZERO WASTE

The City's primary goal is to reduce the environmental impact of waste by minimizing the amount sent to landfills and incinerators.
This is achieved through initiatives like recycling, composting, and waste reduction programs.

SHORT-TERM TARGETS TO BE ACHEIVED BY 2030

1. Reducing municipal solid waste generation per capita by at least 15% compared to 2015.

2. Reducing municipal solid waste disposal to landfill and incineration by at least 50% compared to 2015.

3. Increasing the diversion rate away from landfill and incineration to at least 70%.

* IMPORTANT NOTES ON THE DATA

The City's goals are defined on a municipal-level which encompasses waste management at both residential and commercial levels.

However, the current dashboard focuses solely on residential data, as commercial waste data is not yet available but will be added in the future.
