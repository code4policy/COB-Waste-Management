Team Zero Waste Goals for 2025

Goals for Waste Diversion and Data Visibility

1. Improve Diversion of Trash

  • Increase trash diversion by 5% through:
    1. Reducing trash volumes
    2. Increasing reuse and recovery
    3. Expanding composting
    4. Boosting recycling efforts

    (In that order of priority.)

2. Enhance Data Visibility

  • Develop metrics to identify gaps in our current waste management systems, focusing on:
    1. District/Neighborhood Trends: Understand spatial variations in waste generation.
    2. Seasonal Trends: Identify periods with higher trash volumes to target interventions.
    3. Key Performance Indicators (KPIs): Establish metrics to track stream efficacy.
    4. Existing Local & State Policy: A list of existing policies that help achieve the goal of diversion.
