ZERO WASTE BOSTON

Seasonal Variations

Analyzing seasonal variations over time is essential for effective planning toward meeting the Zero Waste goals by 2025.

Methodology

Monthly averages are compared to the overall average to identify fluctuations and trends in waste management.

Key Insights

Recycling peaks notably in February, while trash disposal rates are highest in August and September, with February showing the lowest disposal levels.
